Thor

hor is a powerful Asgardian warrior and the God of Thunder. He wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, which grants him control over lightning and immense strength. Thor is known for his bravery, and evolving from an arrogant prince to a wise and humble leader.

Loki

Loki is a complex character known as the God of Mischief and the adopted brother of Thor. He possesses shapeshifting abilities and is skilled in sorcery. Loki is notorious for his cunning nature, constantly scheming and manipulating others for his own gain.

Hela

Hela is the Goddess of Death and the primary antagonist in the movie "Thor: Ragnarok." She is the eldest daughter of Odin and the sister of Thor and Loki. Hela is incredibly powerful, with superhuman strength, durability, and the ability to conjure weapons.
